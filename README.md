# We Craft Code

We are a small group of people who leverage learning and programming through motivation, mentoring, and craftsmanship.

Though we're not strict about which bootcamp you should do, we recommend freeCodeCamp.

It simply works, and it's free, as in freedom!

We believe that becoming a developer is much more than the technical skill. We encourage you to share your projects and raise your doubts.

Together we're stronger, we grow stronger!

We grow what companies are looking for: HUMAN BEINGS.

People that are able to communicate, to gather individuals, with empathy and kindness.

As a worldwide community, you'll always have people online, to exchange, discuss or grow ideas with you.

You can even learn techniques such as: pair-programming, rubber-ducking, code review.

All these are encouraged practices by top-tier companies.

We have no color, no race, no sex.
We practice inclusion. We welcome diversity.
You'll be able to use your colleagues slipstream to grow faster, and land your first job in the industry.

100%.
